Favorite Blogs

Here's a collection of blogs that I regularly read and recommend. They cover topics ranging from environmentalism to computer science.

Ecosophia - by John Michael Greer

#environment #futurism #economy

JMG (as he is known among his followers) writes about the depleting reserves of "accessible" natural resources, our future economy (or a lack of thereof), and a critique of our ways. Occasionally, one gets to read about his other passion â€“ spirituality.

Daniel Lemire's blog

#computers #performance #programming

Lemire is a computer scientist and a programmer. He writes about program optimization and performance engineering.

Stephen Diehl's blog

#computers #cryptocurrency #programming

Diehl is a computer scientist and a programmer. He mainly (these days) writes rants against cryptocurrencies. Highly recommended if you have always smelled something fishy about cryptoponz...cryptocurrencies.

Brave (Browser) Blog

#computers #performance #programming

Pretty standard engineering blog from Brave. Great if you'd like to know more about privacy on the web, what brave is doing towards that, and what others are not doing.
